Home (current) Our Mission

Gramiksha is a non-profit organisation registered under M.P. Society Act, 1973. We believe that all children deserve an empowering childhood and equal opportunities. We work to enhance the quality of education in the government schools by assisting them with learning models that encourage critical thinking, equip students with practical knowledge and help in their overall development. We hope to strengthen the public education system and cultivate a culture conducive to learning and growth.
